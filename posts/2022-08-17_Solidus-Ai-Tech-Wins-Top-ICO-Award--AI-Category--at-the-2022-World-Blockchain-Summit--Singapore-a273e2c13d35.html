Solidus Ai Tech Wins Top ICO Award (AI Category) at the 2022 World Blockchain Summit, Singapore

Solidus Ai Tech has reaped a significant win: winning Top Initial Coin offering (AI) at the 2022 World Blockchain Summit in Singapore for its AITECH token, the world’s first artificial intelligence utility token. The 20th global edition of the World Blockchain Summit, one of the most influential Crypto and Blockchain conferences in the world, this year returned to an in-person format, with its focus on Blockchain, Crypto, NFT, the Metaverse, Web 3.0 ecosystems and their far-reaching effects on commerce, culture and communities.

Solidus Ai Tech founder Paul Farhi said: “This is a very important win for us; it acknowledges the uniqueness of our AITECH token. And the event itself is a very important one because it presents us with an unrivalled opportunity to network with some of the most important stakeholders in the Blockchain and Crypto ecosystem — Blockchain and Crypto pioneers, entrepreneurs, investors, government and regulatory bodies, technology leaders developers, industry enthusiasts and the media. It also gives us an opportunity to discuss the ways we can transform the world with this technology.”

In addition to showcasing a variety of Blockchain offerings, the two-day summit is an opportunity to attend high-level plenary talks, presentations and workshops and explore the latest innovations in Blockchain. It attracts thousands participants from across the world, including researchers and industry professionals. Notable speakers at this year’s summit included Jimmy Nguyen, Founding President of BSV Blockchain, Tim Draper, Founder of Draper Associates, Tobias Bauer, Principal at Blockchain Founders Fund, and Anna Tutova, CEO of Coinstelegram.

The global Blockchain market is projected to reach US39.7 billion by 2025, growing at a CAGR of 67.3 per cent.

About the World Blockchain Summit
 The World Blockchain Summit is a global series of elite gatherings held in more a number of destinations across the world. It connects global Blockchain experts and technology players, including emerging start-ups, with regional businesses, government authorities, IT leaders, tech entrepreneurs, investors, and Blockchain developers.

About

Founded in December 2017, Solidus Technologies started as a cryptocurrency mining ﬁrm with a particular focus on mining Ethereum (ETH) via GPU-based mining rigs. In the wake of the 2020 financial crash and the significant boost in demand for AI services, the company shifted its core focus to Artificial Intelligence and incorporated Solidus AI Tech to become the AI arm of the business. Solidus’ Artificial Intelligence infrastructure will enable Government Authorities, Megacorps, SME’s, Professionals, Metaverse and Play2Earn projects to purchase AI services and High Performance Computing Power. Solidus AI Tech is launching its eco-friendly AITECH token to operate seamlessly with their AI infrastructure. AITECH can be bought, staked or held.
