Driving Innovation Forward: Recent Updates in Our GPU Marketplace

In our mission to revolutionize access to GPU computing power, we’re excited to provide an update on our groundbreaking GPU Marketplace. This initiative aims to democratize high-performance GPU usage, allowing innovators, developers, and businesses to leverage advanced computing power without the burden of significant hardware investments. Our progress so far showcases our dedication to this vision and highlights the substantial steps we’re taking to bring it to life.

🔧 Backend Development: The Powerhouse Behind the Scenes

The backend of our GPU Marketplace is the engine that powers everything, from user authentication to the seamless operation of GPU instances. With 85% of the backend development completed, we are steadily progressing towards supporting a robust and scalable marketplace.

  • System Setup: 100% ✅ — Our backend architecture is fully set up and operational.
  • Account Management: 100% ✅ — Secure and efficient account management is in place.
  • Billing: 100% ✅ — Complete billing system ready for transactions.
  • Earning: 100% ✅ — Earnings management system is fully operational.
  • Manage Machine: 80% — Tools for managing GPU machines are almost ready.
  • List Machine: 65% — We’re getting closer to allowing users to list their GPU machines.
  • Rent Machine: 65% — Renting functionalities are shaping up well.
  • Engine Management: 75% — Managing GPU engines is almost complete.
  • Admin: 85% — Admin panel for overseeing operations is nearly finished.
