Decentralized Power: The Heart of DAO

Before diving into the specifics of the burn event, it’s essential to understand the philosophy behind DAO. Instead of decisions being made in exclusive boardrooms, the community — comprising stakeholders — collectively guides the future. The ethos? Shared responsibilities, shared outcomes.
