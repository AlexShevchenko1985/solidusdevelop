Solidus Technologies Announces Partnership With Blockchain Consulting Firm Blocksultancy

LONDON, February 14, 2022

Solidus Technologies has announced a partnership with leading Blockchain consulting firm Blocksultancy to manage the fast expanding Solidus AI Tech community, the AI arm of the technology company.

Blocksultancy’s management will bring a 360-degree view of all ongoing activities in the Solidus AI Tech community, maintain harmonised communications between Solidus AI Tech’s worldwide network of investors and customers, create effective growth strategies to support the development of community and, ultimately, boost return on investment.

Solidus Technologies Founder and Head of UK operations Paul Farhi said: “We’re delighted to have Blocksultancy on board. Partnering with Blocksultancy means that our superb community will get expert, professional management. The team’s significant expertise in accelerating growth will further boost our reach, and they’ll also play an important role in our marketing to large investor groups and venture capitalists.”

According to CoinMarketCap, the current global crypto market cap is $1.89T. As the crypto market continues to grow exponentially, so will the number of cryptocurrency communities will increase. Cryptocurrency community management will gain momentum, tasked with improving the engagement strategies and communications of digital currency companies.

Speaking about the new partnership, Blocksultancy CEO Tom Parsons said: “We are very pleased to have partnered with Solidus AI Tech. Our aim is to help manage their already strong community as well as to implement additional strategies to positively affect the continued growth of the project. Everyone at Blocksultancy is thrilled to be partnering with such a revolutionary, technological innovator. We feel Solidus AI Tech is at the cutting edge of something special, with Artificial Intelligence soon to become a part of the average person’s everyday life. Working with Solidus AI Tech will allow us to partner with like-minded visionaries who will help shape our tomorrow.”

About

Founded in December 2017, Solidus Technologies started as a cryptocurrency mining ﬁrm with a particular focus on mining Ethereum (ETH) via GPU-based mining rigs. In the wake of the 2020 financial crash and the significant boost in demand for AI services, the company shifted its core focus to Artificial Intelligence and incorporated Solidus AI Tech to become the AI arm of the business. Solidus’ Artificial Intelligence infrastructure will enable Government Authorities, Megacorps, SME’s and Professionals to purchase AI services. Solidus AI Tech is launching its eco-friendly AITECH token to operate seamlessly with their AI infrastructure. AITECH can be bought, staked or held.
