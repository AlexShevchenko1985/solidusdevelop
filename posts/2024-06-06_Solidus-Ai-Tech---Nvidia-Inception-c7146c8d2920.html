Solidus Ai Tech — Nvidia Inception

Solidus Ai Tech, headquartered in Dubai, United Arab Emirates, partnered with NVIDIA Inception, a renowned program dedicated to nurturing startups that are revolutionizing various industries with cutting-edge technological advancements. This collaboration marks a significant milestone in Solidus Ai Tech’s journey towards transforming the landscape of artificial intelligence and high-performance computing.

At Solidus Ai Tech, our primary focus is on spearheading innovation in AI and HPC by providing eco-friendly infrastructure and state-of-the-art services. We specialize in offering Artificial Intelligence as a Service (AIaaS), Blockchain as a Service (BaaS), and high-performance computing capabilities, empowering businesses to leverage AI for their applications while maintaining a strong commitment to sustainability. Through the utilization of our deflationary utility token, AITECH, we are establishing a seamless ecosystem where users can seamlessly access, license, and contribute to groundbreaking AI solutions, all while being rewarded for their active participation.

The partnership with NVIDIA Inception presents an exciting opportunity for Solidus Ai Tech to access a wealth of resources designed to accelerate our growth and development. This includes access to comprehensive technical training, preferential pricing on NVIDIA hardware and software, complimentary cloud credits, and invaluable technology assistance. By leveraging these resources, we aim to optimize our AI infrastructure, drive down operational costs, and expedite the development and deployment of innovative AI solutions.

Furthermore, being part of the NVIDIA Inception program enables us to engage in collaborative ventures with industry-leading experts and fellow AI-driven organizations. This collaborative environment facilitates knowledge exchange, fosters potential partnerships, and enhances our market presence, ultimately contributing to the advancement of the AI ecosystem as a whole.

Paul Farhi, CEO of Solidus Ai Tech “Our decision to join NVIDIA Inception underscores our unwavering commitment to pushing the boundaries of AI innovation. By leveraging the invaluable resources offered by the program, such as technical training, preferential pricing, and access to cloud credits, we anticipate accelerating our development cycles and enhancing our operational efficiency. Moreover, the collaborative environment provided by Inception offers us the opportunity to forge strategic partnerships with industry leaders and like-minded innovators, driving mutual growth and fostering a vibrant ecosystem of AI-driven solutions.”

NVIDIA Inception helps startups during critical stages of product development, prototyping and deployment. Every NVIDIA Inception member gets a custom set of ongoing benefits, such as NVIDIA Deep Learning Institute credits, preferred pricing on NVIDIA hardware and software, and technology assistance, which provides startups with the fundamental tools to help them grow.
