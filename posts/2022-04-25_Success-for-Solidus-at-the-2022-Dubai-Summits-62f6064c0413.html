Success for Solidus AI Tech at the 2022 Dubai Summits

LONDON, 25th April, 2022

Attendance at the World of Web3 Summit (WOW) and the World of Blockchain Summit in Dubai earlier this year proved fruitful for representatives of Solidus Technologies, with important partnerships being forged to move the Solidus AI Tech project forward. Solidus AI Tech is now headquartered in Dubai, operating under cryptocurrency licences.

WOW connects global NFT and DeFi leaders with international businesses, government authorities, tech visionaries, investors and developers. This year’s keynote speakers included Sam Katiela, board member at Crypto Oasis, who spoke on “The Future of Humanity and the New Way of Money Making” and Hoda Alkhzaimi, President at Emirates Digital Association for Women, who spoke on “Building Financial Structures for Metaverse Tech Buildup Investment”. Distinguished panellists discussed a range of subjects including the current state of the Blockchain startup ecosystem, GameFi as the next way to innovate with business models and regulated digital asset exchanges as important players on the market.

The World of Blockchain Summit, held at the Atlantis, is widely regarded as the most influential crypto and Blockchain conference in the Middle East. The event, which focuses on all matters of Blockchain, crypto, NFT, the Metaverse, and Web 3.0 ecosystems and their wide-reaching impacts, provides unmatched networking opportunities for investors, government representatives, entrepreneurs, industry enthusiasts and the Media.

Solidus Technologies Founder Paul Farhi, who attended both events, said: “The summits have been very good for our project. We’re currently having important conversations with companies in the Metaverse and Play-to-Earn spaces — they are very excited about our HPC capabilities. We’ve also secured significant venture capital investment and we’re in conversation with a number of other VC and family offices who are keen to benefit from the Solidus AI Tech project.”

Solidus also launched its much-anticipated competition, which will see one lucky investor gifted with either a top-of-the-range car with a price value of $50,000 or $50,000 in cryptocurrency. To participate in or learn more about the competition, head over to Solidus AITECH: The World’s First Artificial Intelligence Utility Token (ai-tech.io) or email competition@ai-tech.io
