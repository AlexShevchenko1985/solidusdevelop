Welcome to the Solidus Ai Tech Friday Roundup with Ava, bringing you the latest and greatest updates from the world of technology!

🌟 SambaNova’s Fortune Future50 Recognition
SambaNova has been recognized as №6 on Fortune’s 2024 Future50 list! This achievement underscores their relentless drive to push AI boundaries and open new doors for customers and the world at large.”

🔥 OpenAI Launches ChatGPT Pro
OpenAI just introduced ChatGPT Pro, a $200/month plan granting unlimited access to all models, including the powerful new ‘o1 reasoning model.’ Ideal for users tackling complex tasks, this premium tier represents a serious upgrade in AI capabilities.

🎥 Tencent’s ‘Hunyuan Video’ Model
Tencent dropped ‘Hunyuan Video,’ an advanced open-source text-to-video AI model boasting 13 billion parameters. It excels at tasks like generating videos from text, animating still images, creating avatars, and adding audio. The results are jaw-dropping, heralding a new era for open-source video generation.

🧙 Google DeepMind’s Genie 2
Google DeepMind unveiled Genie 2, an AI that generates playable 3D worlds. With the ability to create endless, action-controllable environments from a single prompt image, Genie 2 is paving the way for immersive, AI-driven gaming experiences.

🖥️ MythBusters’ CPU vs. GPU Demonstration
Adam Savage and Jamie Hyneman (MythBusters legends) teamed up with NVIDIA to show the difference between CPUs and GPUs.

🌐 World Labs’ Fully Interactive 3D Worlds
Fei-Fei Li’s World Labs introduced an AI system that can generate fully interactive 3D worlds from a single image. This groundbreaking approach offers more control and consistency for content creators in movies, games, and simulations.

🤝Elon Musk Secures Nvidia’s Blackwell GPUs for xAI
Elon Musk secured a $1.08 billion deal to put xAI first in line for Nvidia’s Blackwell GPUs.
