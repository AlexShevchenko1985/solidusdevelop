✨ Follow — Two Easy Steps to be IDO Ready on AITECH Pad!

Step 1: Get yourself started in less than 60 seconds!

  1. Visit www.aitechpad.io
  2. Select Register Now
  3. Create an Account
  4. Enter Verification code from Email

Voila, Congratulations you have now created in an account on AITECH Pad.

Step 2: Get Ready for IDO’s!

  1. Go to ‘Portfolio’
  2. Click on ‘Verify your account’ & Complete the process
  3. Go to ‘Portfolio’ & Click ‘Verify wallet’
  4. Connect your wallet.

🎉 Congratulations, you are now eligible to participate in the IDO’s based on your TIER. What is a TIER? 👇

🎖️ AITECH TIERS

Tier members will relish an array of benefits, such as exclusive airdrops, entry to seed, private sales & public IDOs, and a plethora of other exclusive opportunities!

AITECH Pad offers 6 tier levels Base, Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum and Diamond.

To earn a tier, users must stake AITECH tokens. The staking pools below are boosted pools designed to multiply your staked amount and give you a boost in your tier. However, users who have staked in the pools previously available are also eligible for the tiers based on the staked amount.

How Boosted Staking Pools Work?

  • 90 Day Pool has a +30% Boost
  • 180 Day Pool has a +50% Boost
  • 365 Day Pool has a +100% Boost

If you stake in one of the above mentioned pool, your staked value multiplies, giving you a higher tier based on the amount of $AITECH Staked.

For Example:

You have: 1000 AITECH
You staked in the 365 Day with 100% Boost.
AITECH Pad will upgrade you to a Bronze Tier, as your 1000 AITECH is given a 100% boost, making the total for you as 2000 AITECH

ℹ️ To be able to participate & get benefits: A minimum of 2000 AITECH is required to be staked.
