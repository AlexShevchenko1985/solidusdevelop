What is Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS)?

Cloud computing that offers essential storage, computing, and on-demand networking services is called Infrastructure as a Service IaaS. Transferring your company’s infrastructure to an IaaS solution is very beneficial to your company as it helps in reducing electricity and maintenance costs. IaaS solutions are very cost-effective regarding data centers, hardware, and IT resources. They help you avoid any complications in purchasing and managing physical servers.

Applications of IaaS

IaaS services support a number of applications that include and are not limited to network firewalls & security, servers & storage, operating systems, database management, development tools, business analytics, data centres, and hosted applications. IaaS services help you run new applications efficiently and expand the dependability of your underlying infrastructure.
