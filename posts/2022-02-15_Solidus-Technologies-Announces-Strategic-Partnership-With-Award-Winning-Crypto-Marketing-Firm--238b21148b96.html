Solidus Technologies Announces Strategic Partnership With Award-Winning Crypto Marketing Firm, Crowdcreate

LONDON, February 15, 2022

Solidus Technologies has announced a partnership with pioneering full-service growth marketing agency Crowdcreate, which runs one of the largest networks of crypto founders, investors and thought leaders. Crowdcreate will bring a full suite of marketing and consulting services — from influencer marketing and expert advisory services to content creating, community building and venture capital investing.

Solidus Technologies Founder and Head of UK operations Paul Farhi said: “Crowdcreate has been in the Blockchain industry since 2017, and the company has distinguished itself as a global pioneer in launching and scaling some of the most successful projects in Blockchain and Web 3.0. We will be leveraging Crowdcreate’s significant expertise to help scale our project. We’re delighted to have the Crowdcreate team on board.”

The move also reflects the Solidus’ strong commitment to its rapidly expanding Solidus AI Tech community. Among investors, communities are a means of gauging the likely trajectory of a project.

The partnership with Crowdcreate will augment existing Solidus capabilities. Crowdcreate will bring additional infrastructure and bandwidth, streamline communications over Solidus’ global user base and ensure compliance with local jurisdictions and laws, acting as a gatekeeper to investors who do not meet the stringent requirements set.

Crowdcreate was named “Top Crypto Marketing Firm” by Forbes, CoinBureau, and Clutch. Experts in NFT marketing and crypto marketing, including crypto influencer marketing and crypto investor marketing, the company has worked with some of the largest companies in the world to raise investor funds, rapidly increase sales, create buzz, acquire users and expand their online community. To date the company has raised $133 million, reached 7.1 million people and successfully managed more than 300 projects.

About

Founded in December 2017, Solidus Technologies started as a cryptocurrency mining ﬁrm with a particular focus on mining Ethereum (ETH) via GPU-based mining rigs. In the wake of the 2020 financial crash and the significant boost in demand for AI services, the company shifted its core focus to Artificial Intelligence and incorporated Solidus AI Tech to become the AI arm of the business. Solidus’ Artificial Intelligence infrastructure will enable Government Authorities, Megacorps, SME’s and Professionals to purchase AI services. Solidus AI Tech is launching its eco-friendly AITECH token to operate seamlessly with their AI infrastructure. AITECH can be bought, staked or held.
