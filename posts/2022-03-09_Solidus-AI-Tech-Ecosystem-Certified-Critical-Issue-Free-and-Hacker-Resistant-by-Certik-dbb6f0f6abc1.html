Solidus AI Tech Ecosystem Certified Critical Issue Free and Hacker Resistant by Certik

LONDON, March 9, 2022

Solidus Technologies today announced it had passed and published the results of its Certik audit, widely viewed as the Rolls Royce of audits for smart contracts. A pioneer in Blockchain security, Certik uses advanced artificial intelligence technology to safeguard and monitor blockchain protocols and smart contracts. Today, Certik is one of the fastest-growing companies in blockchain security.

Speaking about the audit, Solidus Technologies founder Paul Farhi said: “Passing the audit is an important step in our growth as a community-driven platform committed to facilitating wealth opportunities for all who invest in our ecosystem. The certification is a strong validation of the robustness of our platform, and this will create even greater confidence in Solidus and its continued growth.”

With Certik, blockchain ecosystems are mathematically verified to be free of critical issues and hacker-resistant. To date, the company has worked with over 2500 enterprise clients, helped secure over $300 billion worth of digital assets and detected over 45,000 vulnerabilities in blockchain code. Clients of the pioneering company include Aave, Polygon, Binance, Smart Chain, Terra, Yearn and Chiliz.
