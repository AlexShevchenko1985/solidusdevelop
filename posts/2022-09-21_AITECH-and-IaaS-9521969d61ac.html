About Solidus AITECH

Solidus Ai Tech is a computation network created to distribute computing power to organizations working on complex AI projects requiring high computing power. They are on a mission to build an eco-friendly High-Performance Computing (HPC) Data Centre and an Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) platform. They aim to assist Governmental Authorities, SMEs, Megacorps and Professionals by providing them with computing power and Artificial Intelligence Licensing services through our cryptocurrency token AITECH.

Our vision supports the development of truly efficient data centres that can fulfill the need for computing power in the global market. AITECH intends to alter how computing activities are managed and performed when joined with scalable tools to help developers safely convey and monetize their applications.

Where Are They With The Current Data Centre?

Solidus Ai Tech have finished building the main Data Centre and are currently preparing to begin the installation of the HPC infrastructure based in Europe (Bucharest). Many European companies faced a big problem with the lack of HPC facilities, making them seek their supercomputing needs overseas. By building and basing the Data Centre in Europe, it made the process easier and more secure for companies to access their supercomputing needs. The long-term plan is to build additional data centres in Europe, thus increasing the revenue streams. Solidus Ai Tech’s facilities will be more efficient and lower in cost, consuming 40% less power than the industry average.

One of the primary goals is to build energy-efficient AI HPC Data Centres. Lowering computation costs will make applications like scientific calculation, CGI rendering, and machine learning more accessible.
