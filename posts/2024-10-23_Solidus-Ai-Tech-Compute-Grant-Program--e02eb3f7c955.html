The Grant Program!

The $1M Compute Credits Grant is designed to empower AI-driven companies in the Web2 and Web3 spaces by providing unparalleled access to essential compute resources. Whether you’re building next-gen AI applications, exploring decentralized AI solutions, or scaling your existing operations, this grant is here to fuel your growth.

Who Should Apply?

If you’re a Web2 AI startup or a Web3 venture at the forefront of AI innovation, this grant could be your catalyst for exponential growth. We are looking for companies with a proven track record or promising concepts that leverage AI in transformative ways creating real world impact on unprecedented scales. Check your eligibility today.

Benefits of the Grant

  • Unmatched Compute Power: Access cutting edge high-performance computing resources tailored for AI workloads.
  • Accelerate Your Growth: Use these resources to scale your operations, train & run complex AI models, and experiment without the financial burden.
  • Connect with Experts: Gain visibility and potential partnership opportunities.

Grant Distribution Structure Tier 1
Developed and Live-Stage Ventures with Users ($500,000 in Compute Credits) 50% of Total Grant

Established companies with a live product in the market, generating revenue or having an active user base. These ventures are focused on scaling their operations, improving their AI-driven offerings, and expanding their market presence.

Example Recipients

  • Web2 AI companies with established products looking to expand features and user capacity or planning to explore Web3 opportunities.
  • Web3 ventures with live decentralized applications (dApps) requiring significant compute power for user engagement and AI processing.

Support Provided

  • Large-scale compute resources to handle growing user demand and optimize AI performance.
  • Advanced support for integrating AI solutions into more complex or diverse environments.
  • Opportunities to collaborate with leading web 3 players and scale globally.
  • Enrollment in Acceleration + launch program by AITECH Pad

Criteria for Selection

  • A live product with active users or customers.
  • Strong growth metrics and a clear strategy for scaling.
  • Proven market demand and the ability to significantly benefit from increased compute capacity.

Grant Distribution Structure Tier 2
Mid-Stage Ventures with MVP (Upto $400,000 in Compute Credits) 40% of Total Grant

Companies that have developed an MVP and are in the process of refining their product, validating their market, and preparing for a larger scale rollout. These ventures have demonstrated their concept’s viability and are working towards scaling.

Example Recipients

  • AI companies who are refining AI models for specific industries.
    Web2/Web3 ventures preparing for a broader market launch with a solid MVP.
  • Web2/Web3 ventures preparing for a broader market launch with a solid MVP.

Support Provided

  • Enhanced compute resources to optimize and scale AI models.
  • Strategic advice on market expansion and product refinement.
  • Access to a broader network of potential partners and customers.
  • Enrollment in Incubation + launch program by AITECH Pad.

Criteria for Selection

  • A working MVP with demonstrable results.
  • Evidence of market traction or customer interest.
  • A clear plan for scaling the product and utilizing the compute credits.

Grant Distribution Structure Tier 3

Early-Stage Ventures ($100,000 in Compute Credits) 10% of Total Grant

Early-stage companies that are in the ideation or initial development phase. These ventures may not yet have a minimum viable product (MVP) but show significant potential in leveraging AI within the Web2 or Web3 spaces.

Example Recipients

  • AI startups currently in the research and development phase.
  • Web3 ventures exploring decentralized AI applications with a compelling idea but limited initial development.

Support Provided

  • Access to compute resources to develop prototypes and train initial models.
  • Mentorship and guidance on scaling AI solutions from concept to MVP.
  • Networking opportunities Web3 potential investors.
  • Advisory and potential Incubation, Acceleration + launch program by AITECH Pad.

Criteria for Selection

  • Innovative AI concepts with a clear vision.
  • Innovative business models that would attract users.
  • Strong founding team with relevant experience.
  • Feasibility of concept and potential for market disruption.
