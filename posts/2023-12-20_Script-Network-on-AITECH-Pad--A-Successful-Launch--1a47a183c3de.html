What is Script Network?

Introduction

Script Network is a layer 1 open source live tv platform, protocol and storage network. One of our core products, Script TV, is the first watch-to-earn live tv platform, built to reward users whilst they watch, provide video dStorage and redefine video advertisement for the crypto industry. Users can earn more rewards and enhance their experience through unique GameFi elements on and off chain.

🌎 Market: Segments

  • TV and Film: $2.5T
  • Video Delivery (CDN): $45B
  • FAST Channel (Free Ad Supported Television) market: $18B

Key Features

  • Watch-to-earn with online TV — watch TV online and get paid rewards per block
  • Redefining crypto advertising (allowing crypto/NFT projects to easily promote via the platform)
  • Gamified Reward Structure — earn rewards through our viewing content, engaging, and upgrading your NFTs
  • Fully Decentralised video storage solution — lowering costs by over 80% for Web2 companies
