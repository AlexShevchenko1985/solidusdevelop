Introduction of Script Network

Script TV is a decentralized video delivery network leveraging blockchain technology to solve issues in traditional video streaming. It offers high-quality streaming and incentivizes decentralized bandwidth and content sharing at lower costs than traditional providers. Users can earn tokens by relaying video content to others watching the same content, making Script Network a pioneering platform in video delivery.

The Script Token (SCPT) serves as the governance token for the Script protocol. It is utilized for staking as a Validator and Lightning Node, facilitating block production and protocol governance within the Script Network. Users who stake and operate nodes can earn a share of newly generated SCPT tokens based on their contribution.
