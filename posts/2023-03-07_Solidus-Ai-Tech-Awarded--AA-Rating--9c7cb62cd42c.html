What’s CAR and why is it important?

CryptoAssetRating.com (CAR) is a fintech company that has set out to bring transparency to the crypto market. CAR is dedicated to providing investors with comprehensive, reliable ratings, research, and insights on crypto assets. The company’s mission is to help investors make informed decisions about their investments by providing them with accurate information about the risks and potential rewards associated with investing in cryptocurrencies.

The ratings framework developed by CAR is an essential tool for investors who want to understand the investment quality of a crypto asset. The ratings are based on the ability and willingness of a crypto asset issuer or company to execute its proposed business plan successfully. The ratings capture the risks associated with investing in a particular asset from different perspectives such as legal, business, technical, and financial.

CAR’s analysts are highly capable and bring a wealth of experience to the ratings process. They use a rigorous methodology to analyze the crypto assets and issuers to provide an accurate and unbiased assessment of the investment quality of each asset. The ratings provided by CAR are not influenced by any outside parties, and the company is committed to maintaining its independence and objectivity.

Solidus Ai Tech Rated “AA”

One of the recent developments at CAR is that they have awarded AITECH, a blockchain-based platform, with an ‘AA rating.’ This rating indicates that the issuer is well positioned and resourced to deliver on its strategic objectives. The issuer has the right technical, financial, legal expertise to navigate hurdles effectively and execute on its product/solution roadmap.

The ‘AA rating’ is one of the highest ratings that CAR offers, and it is a testament to the quality of AITECH’s team and their technology. This rating will undoubtedly help AITECH to attract more investors and build trust in their platform. Additionally, it provides valuable information to potential investors about the quality and reliability of the platform.

but, what is Solidus Ai Tech & what do they offer?

Solidus has built an eco-friendly 8,000 square foot high performance computing (HPC) Data Centre in a highly secure location in Europe. They are launching the World’s first deflationary Artificial Intelligence token (AITECH) which will be used as a payment gateway to licence Artificial-Intelligence-as-a-Service (AIaaS), Blockchain-as-a-Service (BaaS) and rent High Performance Computing (HPC) power via their Infrastructure-as-a-Service (IaaS) platform.

Solidus will also launch a marketplace for software developers to launch Artificial Intelligence software applications where the developers will benefit via a profit share model. AITECH is the token that powers the Solidus AI Tech ecosystem and can be used to purchase everything that is listed on the platform. Fiat currency transactions are converted to AITECH seamlessly on backend and are completed on the Blockchain with AITECH tokens.
