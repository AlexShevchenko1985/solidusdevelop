Solidus Ai Tech appoints Top ICO Experts to Serve as Growth Advisors and provide additional investment

LONDON, March 14, 2022

Solidus Ai Tech today announced that it had appointed a number of top ICO advisors as part of a range of measures to support its growth, which include plans to work with five launch pads and three IEO pads.

Solidus Ai Tech Founder and Head of UK operations Paul Farhi said: “I couldn’t be more pleased with the momentum we’ve generated, from the venture capitalist firms Solidus is attracting and with whom we’re now in conversation right through to the launch pads — out of the $25m private sale from our Aitech token $20m has already been reserved for this.”

Ireland-based Simon Cocking, a senior editor of Irish Tech News and former Chief Editor at CryptoCommonwealth, CryptoCoinNews and InvestinIT, will serve as a key strategy advisor to Solidus. Cocking has worked as a business mentor and advisor to over 200 companies to date and features regularly on numerous global Twitter influencer lists. He was a top-ranked member of People of Blockchain based on total funds raised.

Also advising Solidus is Timo Trippler, an ICO advisor and entrepreneur within fintech. Trippler is an expert in finance and risk management and brings 12 years of experience in financial transactions. He has served as an advisor on numerous successful ICO projects, manages multiple crypto fund portfolios and boasts a network of more than 300 institutional investors around the world, travelling regularly to Blockchain summits and conferences to meet new investors and to enlarge his fundraising network. Trippler’s impressive portfolio of projects includes AirPod, Loyakk, Faxport, Welltrado, Inclusivity, Vegan Nation, Styxr and Run It.

Jason Hung, co-founder of the International Consensus Association and a managing consultant in enterprise software, will also serve as an advisor to Solidus. Hung has held several CEO positions and was senior AVP at iSoftStone, China. He was also rated as top People of Blockchain, top #1 ICO expert by ICOBench and top #9 advisor by ICOHolder — a position he has maintained since 2018.

About

Founded in December 2017, Solidus Technologies started as a cryptocurrency mining ﬁrm with a particular focus on mining Ethereum (ETH) via GPU-based mining rigs. In the wake of the 2020 financial crash and the significant boost in demand for AI services, the company shifted its core focus to Artificial Intelligence and incorporated Solidus AI Tech to become the AI arm of the business. Solidus’ Artificial Intelligence infrastructure will enable Government Authorities, Megacorps, SMEs and Professionals to purchase AI services. Solidus AI Tech is launching its eco-friendly AITECH token to operate seamlessly with its AI infrastructure. AITECH can be bought, staked or held.
