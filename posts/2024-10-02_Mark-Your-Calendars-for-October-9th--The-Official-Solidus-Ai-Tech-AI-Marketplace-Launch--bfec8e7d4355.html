What is the AI TECH AI Marketplace?

The AI Marketplace is more than just a platform — it’s a comprehensive ecosystem designed to democratize access to advanced AI tools. Key Product Segments:

Foundational AI Models: The most widely used open source AI Models (LLMs, Speech-to-text, Multimodal Models,etc.) will be made available to users via two channels:

Pre-deployed models made available through APIs. These models will be powered by the SambaNova stacks powering the models with the fastest inferencing speeds at extremely economic prices.

Self-deployable models. Users can choose from a variety of widely used open source models and deploy them with a few clicks on a GPU from the Compute Marketplace.

AI Agents: Agents built by the Marketplace community on top of the foundational models which can be subscribed to by application and software developers in order to integrate these agents into their solutions.

Standalone AI Applications: Tailored to specific customer requests or generally applicable AI Applications built on top of the previous two layers which can be used by the marketplace users on a subscription or one-time purchase basis
